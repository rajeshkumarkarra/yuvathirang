A project that is directed toward children living through the loss of their parents and/or from an economically deprived background.

NATIONALISM

As firm believers that, a true nationalist is one who holds one's country's best interests at heart and one who contributes in one's little ways every other day, our project on nationalism is inclined in regard that, we work on spreading across the message of true essence of nationalism - among the young and the vulnerable age groups especially

HUMAN DEVELOPMENT

Denying the generalized notion about economics alone holding power to change a nation's fate, Human Development places an emphasis on personal development of individuals through constructive changes on their personal front, towards finding the freedom of voice for an equity in one's rights.

Our Vision & Mission

We have pledged to align our programmes and extend our services consciously on our long term journey from a gradual development towards its sustainability on our social front.

To promote the ideology of effective ways in terms of providence for Human Development, Child Education, Women Development, and Nationalism.

We firmly believe that, raising awareness plays the key catalyst in refining social scenarios not by its motive per se, but by its ability to give birth to new ideology about the types of modalities that can be taken to in their resolving.

kalam bharat

intro

It is undisputable that India possesses the most promising demographic trends. India has the largest population in the world – with some of the best projects on a global scale being led by Indian youth. And as said by Dr. Kalam Himself that “ This resource of the youth is an important building block for transforming India into a developed nation”. This program is established on the ideas & vision of Dr. Kalam.The primary aim is to create Young Leaders for Taking Forward the Visions and Missions of Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam for a Developed India and Inclusive Society Therefore reverenced, Yuva Thirang and Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Center bring forward to you "KALAM BHARAT" 2019 School Adoption program for 350 schools in and around HYDERABAD & RANGAREDDY.
